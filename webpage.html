A little about
our Project

Traveling around the cities and countries of the world, we began to realize that conventional excursions no longer surprise and leave no proper emotions.

A quest is not just an outing. I did not and do not like excursions, I want freedom. Freedom is important to me. And I offer it to you.

You will search, find, lose and go on.
In the quest, you and only you direct the process: where to go, where to turn, which sights you can touch, and which you can't.

It's up to you. You can take a long walk, stopping at cafes, taking pictures, going somewhere off-trail, or you can take the quest purposefully through the points in a shorter time.
